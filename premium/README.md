## Why You Need Premium Packs

The open-source Core Engine handles **generic** foreign trade communication well — it can identify scenarios, generate professional replies, and flag risks.

But for **real business use**, you need:

- Industry-specific technical terminology and parameters
- Product-specific price objection logic
- Company-specific payment terms, warranty policy, and red lines
- Real customer case studies for team training
- Output calibrated to your market, products, and brand voice

**Without packs, the engine is a skeleton. With packs, it's a business tool.**

## Customization Process

### Step 1: Assessment
Tell us about your industry, products, target markets, and current communication challenges.

### Step 2: Information Gathering
We'll request your product catalog, past customer messages, pricing policy, and any internal communication guidelines.

### Step 3: Pack Building
We build structured packs following our open-source schema standards.

### Step 4: Calibration
We test the packs with your real customer messages and calibrate the output until it meets your standards.

### Step 5: Delivery
You receive the packs in markdown format, ready to load into any AI tool (ChatGPT, Claude, WorkBuddy, Custom GPT, etc.)

### Step 6: Ongoing Updates
Packs can be updated as your products, policies, or markets change.
