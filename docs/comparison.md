## What the Free Version Can Do

The open-source Core Engine in Generic Mode can:

- Identify which of 10 trade scenarios a customer message belongs to
- Analyze customer intent (genuine inquiry vs. negotiation tactic vs. complaint)
- Flag commercial risks (don't lower price, don't accept O/A, don't admit liability)
- Generate professional WhatsApp and Email replies
- Suggest follow-up actions
- Identify expressions to avoid

**It cannot:**
- Use industry-specific technical terminology
- Ask the right technical parameters for your specific product
- Explain price differences based on your product's actual components
- Follow your company's real payment and warranty policies
- Enforce your internal red lines
- Learn from your real customer interactions

## When to Upgrade

### You Need an Industry Pack If:
- You sell technical/industrial products where specifications matter
- Customers frequently ask technical questions before buying
- Price objections are about specific components or configurations
- After-sales issues require product-specific diagnosis
- You operate in markets where industry knowledge is a competitive advantage

### You Need a Company Pack If:
- You have a sales team that needs consistent communication
- Your company has specific payment terms, warranty policies, or red lines
- You want to prevent sales reps from making unauthorized promises
- Your products have specific configurations that affect pricing
- You want to encode your brand voice into every customer interaction

### You Need Team Training If:
- You have 3+ sales reps
- You want to standardize communication across the team
- You have valuable customer interaction history to learn from
- You want ongoing calibration as markets and products evolve
