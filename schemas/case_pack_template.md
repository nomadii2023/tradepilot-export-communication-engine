# Customer Case Pack Template

> Use this template to create case-based training packs for the TradePilot Export Communication Engine.
> Real customer cases (anonymized) are the most valuable training asset for sales teams.

## Case Format

Each case should follow this structure:

### Case #[Number]: [Brief Title]

**Scenario:** [One of the 10 core scenarios, or "Other"]

**Context:**
- Product: [Product name/model]
- Customer Country: [Country]
- Customer Type: [Importer/Wholesaler/Contractor/Distributor/Brand Owner/E-commerce/End User]
- Trade Stage: [Development/Inquiry/Quotation/Negotiation/Order Execution/After-sales]
- Channel: [WhatsApp/Email/LinkedIn/Alibaba/Other]
- Order Value: [Approximate, if applicable]

**Customer Message (Original):**
> [Paste the actual customer message — anonymize company names if needed]

**What We Actually Replied:**
> [Paste what was actually sent — the good, the bad, or the learning case]

**Analysis:**
- What worked: [What was effective]
- What didn't work: [What could be improved]
- Outcome: [Customer replied / Order placed / Customer disappeared / Complaint escalated / etc.]
- Lesson learned: [Key takeaway for the team]

**Optimized Reply (if different from actual):**
> [An improved version based on the lesson learned]

## Case Quality Standards

### Good Cases Should Include:
- Actual customer message (not fabricated)
- Real context (country, product, stage)
- Actual outcome (what happened)
- Clear lesson learned

### Cases to Prioritize:
- High-value deals (large orders)
- Difficult situations (complaints, claims, cancellations)
- Cultural/communication challenges
- First-time scenarios the team hasn't handled before
- Both wins and losses

### Cases to Avoid:
- Cases where customer PII is not properly anonymized
- Cases without a clear outcome or lesson
- Overly similar duplicate cases

---

## Anonymization Guidelines

When anonymizing cases:
- Replace customer company name with [Customer] or generic name
- Replace actual prices with approximate ranges (e.g., "$15-20/unit" instead of "$17.50/unit")
- Remove personal email addresses and phone numbers
- Keep country, industry, product type, and scenario type — these are not PII
- Keep the essence of the communication challenge
